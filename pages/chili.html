Pennsylvania Chili

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Beef Broth
  • Kidney Beans
  • Black Beans
  • Green Pepper
  • Onion

Cooking Directions

  1. Brown beef in bottom of large stock pot
  2. Chop onion and pepper add to beef while cooking
  3. Add tomato sauce/paste and beef broth, reduce heat to low
  4. Add beans and simmer for 1-3 hrs (depending on how hungry you are)
