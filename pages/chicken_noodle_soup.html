Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs (6)
  • Water (3 qts)
  • Carrots (1 lb)
  • Celery (1 head)
  • Chicken Boullion Cube (6-7)
  • Pasta (1 lb)
  • Pepper (to taste)

Cooking Directions

  1. Bring water to a boil over high heat
  2. Drop in boullion cubes
  3. When heavy boil: Add raw chicken thighs
  4. Shortly after add carrots and celery
  5. Let boil for 30 mins
  6. Pull out chicken, remove skin and bone, then add back to soup
  7. Add pasta and continue boil until pasta is fully cooked
  8. Add pepper and other spices at any time
